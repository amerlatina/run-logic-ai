🔐 Privacy Policy — Spotify & Email Integrations
Last Updated: November 2025
This Privacy Policy describes how Run Logic GPT ("the GPT") uses and processes data when you interact with it through OpenAI's ChatGPT platform, specifically in connection with its Spotify and (planned) email actions.
1. What Data Is Processed
When you ask Run Logic GPT to create a playlist or take a Spotify-related action, the following may be sent to Spotify's API in order to fulfill your request:
Your search queries, genre, and tempo/BPM preferences
Playlist names and descriptions you provide
Your Spotify account ID (required by Spotify to create/modify playlists on your account)
Run Logic GPT does not access, request, or store your Spotify password or login credentials. Authentication happens directly between you and Spotify through Spotify's official OAuth process.
📧 Email (planned feature — not yet active): once live, this section will describe what data (e.g., recipient address, plan/playlist summary content) is sent to SendGrid to deliver emails, and will be updated before the feature is enabled.
2. Data Storage & Retention
Run Logic GPT does not retain playlist content, Spotify account data, or email addresses after your session ends. Any information used to complete an action is processed only for the duration needed to fulfill that request.
If Run Logic GPT is later updated to use a backend service to bridge OpenAI Actions to Spotify or SendGrid, this policy will be updated to disclose any request logging performed by that backend, including retention period.
3. Third-Party Services
This GPT integrates with:
Spotify Web API — for searching tracks and creating/managing playlists on your behalf.
Spotify's Privacy Policy: https://www.spotify.com/legal/privacy-policy/
SendGrid API (planned) — for sending plan/playlist summary emails.
SendGrid's Privacy Policy: https://sendgrid.com/policies/privacy/
Each third-party service processes data under its own privacy policy once it receives your request. We encourage you to review those policies directly.
4. Your Rights & Choices
Because Run Logic GPT does not retain your personal data after a session, there is typically nothing stored on our end to access, correct, or delete. For data held by Spotify (e.g., playlists created, account info) or SendGrid, please contact those platforms directly or manage permissions through your account settings on each service. You can revoke Run Logic GPT's access to your Spotify account at any time via Spotify's app permissions settings.
5. Children's Privacy
Run Logic GPT is not intended for use by children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from users under this age.
6. International Users
If you access Run Logic GPT from outside the United States, your data will be processed via OpenAI's, Spotify's, and SendGrid's infrastructure, which may involve international data transfers. Our basis for processing your data is your consent, given when you authorize an integration via OAuth.
7. Changes to This Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect new features (such as the email integration going live) or changes in how data is handled. The "Last Updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
8. Contact
Questions or concerns about this policy can be sent to support@runlogicapp.com.
